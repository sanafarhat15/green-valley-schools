Complete Admission Process Guide

10 Simple Steps for Admission

Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry

Parents or guardians can visit the school campus or submit an online inquiry form to receive detailed admission information and guidelines.

Step 2: Collect Admission Form

Collect the admission form from the school office or download it from the official website.

Step 3: Fill Application Form

Complete the application form carefully with accurate student and parent details.

Step 4: Submit Required Documents

Submit photocopies of Birth Certificate, Aadhaar Card, Previous School Report Card, Passport Size Photographs, and Transfer Certificate (if applicable).

Step 5: Entrance Assessment

Students applying for Grade 6 and above may be required to appear for a written assessment test.

Step 6: Parent-Student Interaction

An interaction session will be conducted with the Principal or Admission Committee.

Step 7: Admission Confirmation

Selected students will receive an official admission confirmation letter.

Step 8: Meet School Office for Fee Details

Parents must visit the school office to understand fee structure, payment schedule, transport fees, and other applicable charges.

Step 9: Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee, tuition fee, and other applicable charges at the school office or through approved payment methods.

Step 10: Orientation & Class Allotment

Students attend orientation program, receive ID cards, books list, timetable, and class allotment details.
