Safety With Us

At Green Valley High School, student safety is our top priority. Our campus is secured with 24/7 CCTV surveillance and trained security staff. Visitor entry is monitored and recorded.

We conduct regular fire drills, emergency preparedness training, and awareness programs on personal safety and cyber safety.

Our school buses are GPS enabled and monitored for safe transportation. All staff members are trained in child protection policies.

School Rules & Discipline

  • Students must wear proper school uniform.
  • Maintain punctuality and regular attendance.
  • Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students.
  • No use of mobile phones inside campus.
  • Maintain cleanliness in classrooms.
  • Follow examination rules strictly.
  • Bullying or misconduct will not be tolerated.
  • Parents must inform school in case of absence.
